Age Shifter

Instructions

  • Arrow Keys: Move left and right, and jump.
  • Number Keys (1, 2, 3): Switch between ages.
  • 1 - Child: Smallest and fastest, can fit through tight gaps.
  • 2 - Adult: Strongest jumper.
  • 3 - Elder: Can see and interact with mysterious 'ghost' platforms (the faint white blocks).
  • Boss Level Controls:
  • X Key: Enter or exit the turret.
  • Arrow Keys (Up/Down): Aim the turret.
  • Spacebar: Fire the turret.

Hint: To climb unclimbable walls, you can alternate between child and adult fast.
